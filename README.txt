Lights that follow the sun, plus motion-aware auto-off without a single Logic variable.

Lumina shifts colour temperature and brightness throughout the day based on where the sun actually is at your location, so mornings feel like mornings and evenings feel like evenings. Pick a Homey Zone in the app settings, choose a curve preset, done. Sensible defaults, a 24-hour preview before you save, per-zone and per-lamp overrides for the rooms that need something different, and a dashboard widget that flips between Off, Adaptive and Night with one tap.

For motion-controlled rooms, wire a sensor's "no motion" event to the "Dim light & auto-off" flow card: Lumina drops the lamp to a partial brightness, holds for N seconds, then fades smoothly to off. A new motion event in the hold window cancels the auto-off and snaps the lamp back to the adaptive value. Defaults (hold seconds, fade duration, dim mode) live in the app settings, so most motion flows only need to point at the lamp — change the numbers once and every flow follows.
