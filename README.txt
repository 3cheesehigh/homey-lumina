Lights that follow the sun. Lumina shifts colour temperature and brightness throughout the day based on where the sun actually is at your location, so mornings feel like mornings and evenings feel like evenings — without flows, schedules, or per-lamp configuration.

Pick a Homey Zone in the app settings, choose a curve preset, done. Sensible defaults out of the box, a 24-hour preview before you save, per-zone overrides for the rooms that need something different, and a dashboard widget that flips between Off, Adaptive and Night with one tap.
